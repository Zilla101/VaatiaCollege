Admissions Overview

Routine Admissions

We routinely admit students into Basic 7 every year, with a maximum of 60 students per set to ensure quality instruction.

Merit Based

Admission is based strictly on academic merit evaluatd through an entrance examination and oral interview.

Equal Opportunity

No pupil will be disadvantaged by the school by virtue of gender, race, religion or socio-economic status.

Admissions Policy

The College selects for entry those children who have the potential to benefit from the education the College offers. Our process is consistent, fair, and monitored regularly to ensure excellence.

Criteria for Entry

  • Minimum age of 11 years for Basic 7 entry.
  • Aptitude and ability evaluation via entrance assessment.
  • Positive references from the candidate's previous school.
  • Availability of space in the requested class.

Admissions Procedure

01

Online Application: Fill the form at parents.vaatiacollege.com.ng with a N10,000 application fee.

02

Documentation: Complete the form online with passport photographs and birth certificate.

03

Age Check: Candidates must be 11, 12, or 14 years for Basic 7, Basic 8, and SSS 1 respectively.

04

Entrance Examination: Conducted in Makurdi or Abuja centers as assigned.

05

CBT Assessment: Computer Based Test in Mathematics, English, and General Knowledge.

06

Oral Interview: Conducted immediately following the successful CBT assessment.

Entrance Exam Notice: The next Entrance Examination into Basic 7 is slated for Saturday, 21st February, 2026. Closing date for application is Wednesday, 18th February, 2026.

Frequently Asked Questions

Enrollment Fee?

Yes, an enrollment fee of ₦10,000 is paid before you can fill and submit the application form.

Meal Systems?

Students are fed four times a day with healthy foods prepared in our modern kitchen facilities.

Class Size?

Our classes are designed for a maximum of 30 students to maintain a 7:1 student-teacher ratio.

Visiting Days?

No set visiting days, but parents can visit for academic tracking and medical follow-up by appointment.

Acceptance?

A place is accepted only when a signed letter and a non-refundable deposit of N200,000 are received.

Boarding?

Yes, Vaatia College is 100% boarding with standard and hygienic boarding facilities for all students.

Vaatia College Makurdi

Vaatia College is a privately owned school established in 2004. A small class size of 30 students per class and a student/teacher ratio of 7:1 encourages an effective student-teacher relationship.
