Hostel Excellence

Modern Infrastructure

Accommodation is 100% boarding with modern hostels. We operate a water treatment plant ensuring clean and safe water for drinking and domestic use, alongside solar inverters and panels for guaranteed 24/7 electric supply.

Security & Safety

Separate hostels for boys and girls within the College premises. We maintain a friendly, caring environment with a strict anti-bullying policy.

Laundry Services

College staff handle major laundry tasks. Students learn responsibility by washing their inner wears, sports wears, and bed sheets.

Dining Excellence

Meals are prepared by the College under the supervision of a qualified caterer. Students dine four times daily in the dining hall.

  • Nutritious Nigerian cuisine.
  • Structured meal times for etiquette.
  • Community-focused dining environment.
Policy: Outside food items are strictly prohibited; an all-round nutrition is provided on campus.

Essential Requirements

01

No electrical or electronic devices allowed items.

02

No food provisions of any kind allowed inside hostels.

03

No cash in any currency is allowed on student property.

04

All items must be clearly labelled with student’s name.

05

All medication must be handed over to the medical unit.

06

Students must carry suitcases from check-in to hostels.

07

Unlabelled items will not be accepted at resumption.

Vaatia College Makurdi

Vaatia College is a privately owned school established in 2004. A small class size of 30 students per class and a student/teacher ratio of 7:1 encourages an effective student-teacher relationship.
