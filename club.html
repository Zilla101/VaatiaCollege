Co-Curricular Activities

The College provides a wide range of clubs to help students develop social skills, leadership qualities, and personal talents.

Electronics Club

Exploring circuit design, robotics, and practical electronics projects.

Drama Club

Fostering creativity through acting, scriptwriting, and stage production.

Chess Club

Developing strategic thinking, patience, and problem-solving skills.

JETS Club

Junior Engineers, Technologists and Scientists - for aspiring innovators.

Literary & Debating

Enhancing public speaking, argumentation, and literary appreciation.

Press Club

Reporting on school events and developing journalistic skills.

Computer Club

Diving into coding, software applications, and digital literacy.

Vaatia College

Vaatia College is a privately owned school established in 2004. A small class size of 30 students per class and a student/teacher ratio of 7:1 encourages an effective student-teacher relationship.
