Vocational Mastery

Equipping students with practical, high-value technical and vocational skills that guarantee self-reliance.

Students View Programs

Building Self-Reliant Leaders

Our skills acquisition courses encourage learners to be self-reliant and useful within society. These skills remain valuable to students throughout their lives, providing opportunities for entrepreneurship and creative expression.

Practical Training

Hands-on workshops led by professional instructors who guide students through real-world projects and techniques.

Exhibition & Sales

Students showcase and sell their creations at our annual Exhibition Night, gaining entrepreneurial experience.

Lifelong Value

Skills that students can use throughout their lives for personal fulfillment or professional income generation.

Available Programs

Hats & Beads Making

This workshop training provides students with skills to become confident bead and hat makers. It is relatively cost-effective but profitable, with products spanning all kinds of bead artworks.

  • Flower vases and decorative pieces
  • Necklaces, earrings, and bracelets
  • Boater hats to fascinator hats
  • Custom jewelry designs

Dress Making

This high-demand skill acquisition program teaches students how to design and sew clothes under the guidance of a professional tailor. Students are encouraged to be creative in their designs and produce trendy styles.

  • Pattern design and cutting
  • Men's and women's wear
  • Trendy fashion styles
  • Exhibition and sales opportunities

Carpentry & Woodwork

Students are extensively taught everything about woodwork, starting from how to make a piece from scratch, joining pieces, framing, and finishing. Their products cover a wide range of functional furniture.

  • Cushions and upholstered furniture
  • Dressing mirrors and frames
  • Kitchen cabinets and storage
  • Bed frames and laundry drawers

Knitting

Students are taught how to knit by hand as well as with knitting machines to create beautiful and attractive pieces with different patterns. These colorful creations are sold at Exhibition Night.

  • Head warmers and beanies
  • Sweaters and pullovers
  • Socks and mufflers
  • Various knitting patterns and techniques

Creative & Visual Arts

Students are taught how to create masterpieces with their artistic strokes. This skill acquisition adapts various visual art styles to help students express their creativity and develop their artistic talents.

  • Portrait drawing and painting
  • Landscape artwork
  • Abstract art compositions
  • Print making techniques

Technical Drawing

Students are taught how to be precise and detailed in composing drawings that visually communicate how something functions or is constructed. A professional drafter guides students to excellence.

  • Familiar symbols and perspectives
  • Units of measurement and notation systems
  • Visual styles and page layout
  • Professional drafting techniques

Photography and Videography

Capture the world through a lens. Our students learn the art of visual storytelling, mastering camera techniques, lighting, and cinematic composition to produce stunning imagery and films.

  • Digital camera operations and settings
  • Lighting and set composition
  • Video editing and post-production
  • Creative visual storytelling

Leather Works

Learn the craft of creating durable and stylish leather goods. From wallets to belts and footwear, students master the techniques of cutting, stitching, and finishing high-quality leather products.

  • Leather selection and preparation
  • Hand and machine stitching
  • Pattern making for accessories
  • Embossing and finishing techniques

Automobile Repairs

Develop practical mechanical skills in our automobile workshop. Students gain hands-on experience in engine maintenance, troubleshooting, and the fundamental mechanics of modern vehicles.

  • Engine diagnostics and maintenance
  • Braking and suspension systems
  • Automotive electrical basics
  • Preventive vehicle care

Electronics Repairs

Understand the inner workings of modern gadgets. This skill teaches students how to diagnose and repair common electronic devices, fostering a deep understanding of circuitry and components.

  • Circuit board analysis and soldering
  • Component testing and replacement
  • Small appliance troubleshooting
  • Digital electronics fundamentals

Music Skill

Explore the rhythm and melody of musical expression. Students are guided in mastering various musical instruments and vocal techniques, building confidence and artistic flair.

  • Instrument proficiency (Keyboard/Guitar)
  • Music theory and composition
  • Vocal training and performance
  • Ensemble and teamwork skills

Farming Practice

Connect with the earth and learn sustainable agricultural techniques. Our farming program covers crop cultivation, livestock management, and the business of modern agriculture.

  • Soil preparation and irrigation
  • Crop rotation and pest control
  • Livestock care and management
  • Agribusiness and marketing

Data Analysis

Unlock the power of information. Students learn how to collect, process, and interpret data using modern tools, preparing them for the data-driven world of the future.

  • Statistical analysis basics
  • Data visualization techniques
  • Spreadsheet and software tools
  • Drawing insights from datasets

Hardware Repairs

Focus on the physical components of computing. This course covers PC assembly, hardware upgrades, and the diagnosis of physical faults in computer systems.

  • PC assembly and disassembly
  • Hardware diagnostic tools
  • Peripheral device maintenance
  • System upgrades and installation

Software Development and Coding

Learn the language of the future. Our coding program introduces students to programming fundamentals, web development, and the logic behind software creation.

  • Programming logic (Python/JS)
  • Web development (HTML/CSS)
  • Problem-solving through code
  • App development fundamentals

Graphic Design

Design visual content that communicates and inspires. Students master design principles and digital tools to create logos, flyers, and digital artwork.

  • Color theory and typography
  • Layout design and composition
  • Digital design software skills
  • Branding and visual identity

Annual Exhibition Night

At the end of every academic year, students showcase their creations at our prestigious Exhibition Night. This event provides students with real-world experience in presenting and selling their work, fostering entrepreneurial skills and confidence.

Vaatia College Makurdi

Vaatia College is a privately owned school established in 2004. A small class size of 30 students per class and a student/teacher ratio of 7:1 encourages an effective student-teacher relationship.
