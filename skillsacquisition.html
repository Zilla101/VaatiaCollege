Building Self-Reliant Leaders

Our skills acquisition courses encourage learners to be self-reliant and useful within society. These skills remain valuable to students throughout their lives, providing opportunities for entrepreneurship and creative expression.

Practical Training

Hands-on workshops led by professional instructors who guide students through real-world projects and techniques.

Exhibition & Sales

Students showcase and sell their creations at our annual Exhibition Night, gaining entrepreneurial experience.

Lifelong Value

Skills that students can use throughout their lives for personal fulfillment or professional income generation.

Available Programs

Hats & Beads Making

This workshop training provides students with skills to become confident bead and hat makers. It is relatively cost-effective but profitable, with products spanning all kinds of bead artworks.

  • Flower vases and decorative pieces
  • Necklaces, earrings, and bracelets
  • Boater hats to fascinator hats
  • Custom jewelry designs

Dress Making

This high-demand skill acquisition program teaches students how to design and sew clothes under the guidance of a professional tailor. Students are encouraged to be creative in their designs and produce trendy styles.

  • Pattern design and cutting
  • Men's and women's wear
  • Trendy fashion styles
  • Exhibition and sales opportunities

Carpentry & Woodwork

Students are extensively taught everything about woodwork, starting from how to make a piece from scratch, joining pieces, framing, and finishing. Their products cover a wide range of functional furniture.

  • Cushions and upholstered furniture
  • Dressing mirrors and frames
  • Kitchen cabinets and storage
  • Bed frames and laundry drawers

Knitting

Students are taught how to knit by hand as well as with knitting machines to create beautiful and attractive pieces with different patterns. These colorful creations are sold at Exhibition Night.

  • Head warmers and beanies
  • Sweaters and pullovers
  • Socks and mufflers
  • Various knitting patterns and techniques

Creative & Visual Arts

Students are taught how to create masterpieces with their artistic strokes. This skill acquisition adapts various visual art styles to help students express their creativity and develop their artistic talents.

  • Portrait drawing and painting
  • Landscape artwork
  • Abstract art compositions
  • Print making techniques

Technical Drawing

Students are taught how to be precise and detailed in composing drawings that visually communicate how something functions or is constructed. A professional drafter guides students to excellence.

  • Familiar symbols and perspectives
  • Units of measurement and notation systems
  • Visual styles and page layout
  • Professional drafting techniques

Annual Exhibition Night

At the end of every academic year, students showcase their creations at our prestigious Exhibition Night. This event provides students with real-world experience in presenting and selling their work, fostering entrepreneurial skills and confidence.

Vaatia College

Vaatia College is a privately owned school established in 2004. A small class size of 30 students per class and a student/teacher ratio of 7:1 encourages an effective student-teacher relationship.
