Facilities & Activities

Our comprehensive sporting facilities keep learners physically and mentally fit. Students are encouraged to bring proper equipment to fully participate.

Field Sports

One Football pitch for training and inter-house competitions.

Court Games

Two Basketball courts, Two Tennis courts, Two Volleyball courts, and Two Handball courts.

Indoor Games

Facilities for Table Tennis (4 boards), Badminton (2 courts), and Squash (2 courts).

Strategy Games

Mental fitness through Chess (4 boards), Scrabble (4 sets), and Darts (2 boards).

Note: Balls and shuttlecocks are provided by the College. Personal equipment (shoes, rackets, boots) is required.

Vaatia College Makurdi

Vaatia College is a privately owned school established in 2004. A small class size of 30 students per class and a student/teacher ratio of 7:1 encourages an effective student-teacher relationship.
