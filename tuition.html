Payment Methods

Accepted Payment Options

  • Cash Deposits: At any Access Bank Plc branch nationwide.
  • Cheque Payments: Payable to the respective College accounts.
  • Direct Transfers: Online or bank transfers to the designated accounts.

Important: Please ensure you quote your child's full name as the depositor when making payments.

Payment Policies

01

Pre-Check-In Payment: All fees must be paid before students are checked into the hostel.

02

Resumption Deadline: Inability to pay fees on or before the official resumption date may lead to forfeiting your ward's space.

03

No Cash on Premises: Cash payments within the College premises are strictly not permitted.

04

Depositor Information: Always quote your child's full name when making deposits or transfers.

05

Deposit Slips: Customized Access Bank deposit slips are available at the College office for your convenience.

06

Payment Confirmation: Keep all payment receipts and transaction confirmations for your records.
